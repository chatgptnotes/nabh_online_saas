# Digital Marketing Calendar (7 Days)

**Goal:** Increase brand awareness and hospital occupancy.
**Channels:** Instagram (IG), LinkedIn (LI), YouTube (YT).

## Week 1 Strategy
- **Hope & Ayushman:** Focus on trust, facilities, and patient success.
- **Dr. Murali (Personal):** Focus on leadership, medical expertise, and discipline.

---

### Day 1: Monday (Motivation & Leadership)
*   **Channel:** LinkedIn (Personal)
*   **Theme:** The discipline of a Surgeon-Coder.
*   **Content:** A photo of you at your desk or in scrubs. Caption about how coding precision helps surgical precision.
*   **Channel:** Instagram (Hospitals)
*   **Theme:** "We are ready for you."
*   **Content:** Reel/Photo of the morning huddle or the front desk team smiling. "Ready to serve Nagpur."

### Day 2: Tuesday (Patient Success)
*   **Channel:** YouTube Shorts / IG Reels (Hospitals)
*   **Theme:** Recovery Journey.
*   **Content:** A short video testimonial from a discharged patient (with consent) or a "Before/After" of a complex case (anonymized X-rays).
*   **Caption Hook:** "Walking home pain-free today. #HopeHospital #Orthopedics"

### Day 3: Wednesday (Education/Awareness)
*   **Channel:** Instagram / YouTube (Personal & Hospital)
*   **Theme:** "Doc, my knee hurts."
*   **Content:** 60-second explainer video: "3 signs you need to see an ortho." Simple, high-value advice.
*   **Call to Action:** "Book a consultation at Ayushman today."

### Day 4: Thursday (Behind the Scenes)
*   **Channel:** Instagram Stories (Hospitals)
*   **Theme:** Technology & Safety.
*   **Content:** Tour of the OT or showing a piece of advanced equipment. "State-of-the-art care for our patients."

### Day 5: Friday (Team Culture)
*   **Channel:** LinkedIn (Hospital Page)
*   **Theme:** Employee Spotlight.
*   **Content:** Photo of a nurse or staff member who went above and beyond this week. "Heroes of Hope."

### Day 6: Saturday (Community Health)
*   **Channel:** Instagram Post
*   **Theme:** Local Health.
*   **Content:** Infographic: "Dengue/Malaria cases are rising in Nagpur - Stay Safe." (Timely local relevance).

### Day 7: Sunday (Rest & Reflection)
*   **Channel:** LinkedIn / Instagram (Personal)
*   **Theme:** Family & Balance.
*   **Content:** A personal photo with family or a hobby. "Recharging to save lives tomorrow."
