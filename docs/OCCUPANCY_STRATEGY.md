# Goal: 75% Occupancy (Hope & Ayushman Hospitals)

**Objective:** Increase patient inflow and retention to achieve consistent 75% bed occupancy.

## 1. Digital Presence & Marketing
- **Website Audit:** Ensure nabh.online and hospital sites are SEO optimized.
- **Social Media:** Regular posts (Reels/Shorts) featuring success stories, doctor interviews, and health tips.
- **Google Maps:** Optimize GMB profiles. Reply to all reviews within 24h. Drive 5-star reviews from discharged patients.

## 2. Sales & Outreach
- **Recruitment:** Hire dedicated Patient Relationship Officers (PRO) / Sales staff.
  - *Task:* Draft JD for Sales Executive (Hospital Marketing).
- **Referral Network:**
  - Map local clinics (10km radius).
  - Regular visits by PROs to referring doctors.
- **Corporate:** Tie-ups with Nagpur businesses for health checkup camps.

## 3. Patient Experience
- **Feedback Loop:** Daily rounds to check patient satisfaction.
- **Discharge Process:** Smooth, fast discharge to leave a good last impression.

## 4. Analytics
- **Daily Report:** Track daily census (Admission vs Discharge).
- **Conversion:** Track OPD to IPD conversion rates.
